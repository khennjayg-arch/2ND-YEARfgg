Nice (Chinese: 奈斯 Nài sī) is a major character in the To Be Hero X donghua series, having a minor-yet-pivotal role in its first and final arcs, and antagonistic ones in its seventh, eighth, ninth, and tenth arcs. The fifteenth-ranked hero, he seemingly commits suicide at the start of the series, setting its first arc into motion as Lin Ling gets forced to assume his identity.
